Chapter 1-7

Chapter 7 (and before) Thoughts

I'm kind of conflicted on Bianca as a character. I'm glad that compared to other OI, it doesn't appear that after reincarnation/time travel she's figured everything out and is just a genius girl boss who can coast to victory with schemes or secret powers. But, despite (seemingly) having gone through a great deal of hardship, I don't like how she still carries that noble air of entitlement (although it is quite fitting).

I don't condone her whipping a maid, it felt out of left field for me. And although I don't adore when OI fl's become lovey dovey and kind to staff that mistreat them, I also don't really love when they're abusive (but I guess that's kind of an issue I have with OI in general).

But I also can see where she's coming from. Being wed off as a kid, going through years of neglect, hatred, isolated, and even coming back still feeling all that - it must suck. I hope that throughout the story she can grow more as a character.

That said, on the other end, I do like Zachary a lot. I don't think I've seen enough of him yet. But he's certainly hot, and I enjoy the start of him seemingly softening up to Bianca. I just hope it isn't a complete immediate shift.
